Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Syllabus Helper ("we", "our", "the Service") is a tool that helps students manage course syllabi. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and name when you register. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • Syllabus files: PDF, DOCX, or TXT files you upload. These are processed to extract deadlines, grading, and policies. The raw text is encrypted at rest.
  • Authentication tokens: Stored locally in your browser to keep you signed in.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the Service: parsing syllabi, displaying deadlines, answering questions about your courses.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on our server and isolated per user — you can only access your own data.
  • Syllabus text is encrypted using AES-128 (Fernet) encryption at rest.
  • Communication is encrypted via HTTPS (TLS).
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.

Chrome Extension

The Syllabus Helper Chrome extension:

  • Stores your authentication token in Chrome's local storage to keep you signed in.
  • Communicates only with syllabushelper.net — no data is sent to any other server.
  • Does not track your browsing activity.
  • Does not execute any remote code.

Data Deletion

You can delete your uploaded syllabi at any time from the Dashboard. To delete your account entirely, contact us at the email below.
